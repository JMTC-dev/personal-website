Yonder

Windows .NET 8 WPF in development

Yonder is a screen break app for Windows that helps you rest your eyes and work in a healthier rhythm.

  • Gentle eye-rest reminders, following the 20-20-20 rule.
  • Smart pause, so it waits for a natural break and never interrupts you mid-task.
  • Optional on-device posture and blink detection.
  • Focus sessions for when you need to lock in.
  • Simple stats on how you're doing.

Everything runs on your computer. No cloud, no account, no tracking.
