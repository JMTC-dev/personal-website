Why I'm writing here

I figured I'd start writing some of this down.

If something I've worked out helps someone else even a little, then why not put it out there. And honestly, sitting down and trying to put a thought into actual words is good practice on its own. It's harder than it sounds, and it usually sorts out my own head in the process.

A little bit about me: I like to build and tinker. And I still think it's kind of magic that you can have an idea, sit down, build it, and millions of people can end up using the same thing at once. One person, one idea, out there in front of the whole world. It's genuinely like magic to me, and it never really gets old.

My day job is in cyber security, which keeps me thinking about all the scenarios, planning a thing out from A to Z and every way it could go sideways. It's its own kind of building, and it's made me better at the regular kind too.

So this'll mostly be me thinking out loud: stuff I'm building, stuff I'm breaking, and whatever I'm into at the moment. Lately that's maths, a little every day, the slow kind that adds up.

No schedule, Just notes, in case any of it's handy to you too.

Thanks for reading.
